Independent members (each researched on its own)

  • method:news-evidence 0.03 — genuine 2026 momentum (DeepMind AlphaProof Nexus ~9 Erdős proofs; new Navier–Stokes singularity families) but all adjacent/lower-tier, not Millennium resolutions; prediction markets target 2030+ (~55% by 2030), not 2027, and only require "solve", not broad acceptance.
