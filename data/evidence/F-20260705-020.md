## Why the re-run
Methodology fix: the prior forecast's ensemble members were generated in one correlated
reasoning pass and median-pooled onto the base rate. This re-run uses **genuinely
independent isolated subagents**, including a deliberately **bold inside-view maximalist**
told to set the base rate aside and reason from the AI-acceleration mechanism.

- `method:base-rate-only` **0.02** — 1 solved+accepted in 26y across the Clay set; acceptance lags solving by years, so both must fit an ~18-month window.

- `method:skeptic` **0.01** — hardest problems resist all approaches; every current "claim" is crankery; AI-Erdős is a category apart from a Millennium problem.

## Finding

**robust**, not base-rate-hugging by construction. The bold member independently reached
the acceptance-lag wall. Committed pool (median) ≈ **0.03**, slightly below the prior 0.04
because the independent bold member (0.04) was *less* aggressive than the earlier inline
structural member (0.09) — evidence the earlier ensemble was, if anything, mildly
over-confident here, not under-bold.
